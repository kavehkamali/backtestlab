Snapshot — early April 2026, when hypothetical strategy posts flood social feeds—learn to verify

Many viral backtests assume you buy the close and sell the open with no slip—markets do not work that way. Adding a few basis points per side changes compounding dramatically over hundreds of trades. When you test rules on IWM-like universes, impact costs dominate faster than on SPY. Taxes and borrow for shorts are another layer retail sims ignore.
