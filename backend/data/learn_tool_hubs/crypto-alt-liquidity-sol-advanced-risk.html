Key takeaways

  • Liquidity is local: The coin you buy on one venue may not exit easily elsewhere.
  • This week: Volatility clusters can widen spreads—assume worse fills than the chart suggests.
  • High risk: Many altcoins go to zero; treat as tuition budget only.

Snapshot — early April 2026, when crypto equity proxies and BTC volatility still spill into risk sentiment

Altcoins often trade like venture equity with 24/7 marks: narratives shift fast, unlock schedules matter, and liquidity can disappear on Sunday nights. Using SOL as a teaching example, study how network usage metrics relate to token velocity—but remember metrics can be gamed and narratives can detach.

Compare depth on major pairs versus exotic pairs; imagine selling 2% of daily volume versus 0.02%—your realized price changes. This is why professionals stress sizing and why Equilima treats crypto education as risk education first.

Bull case — how this lens can help

Liquidity awareness prevents fantasy fills; you learn to respect order books.

On-chain metrics can complement price—when interpreted skeptically.

Bear case — where it breaks

Exchange listings, hacks, and regulatory bans can gap prices beyond any on-chain story.

Correlation to BTC can spike to ~1 in crashes—diversification may fail exactly when needed.

Bottom line

If you explore alt markets, write down maximum loss you can afford—then assume you lose it. Anything else is self-deception, not research.
