Cash flow is where accounting optimism goes to confess. Capital returns are not automatically shareholder-friendly. Buybacks at peak multiples or debt-funded repurchases can flatter EPS while raising fragility. When evaluating JPM, pair repurchase dollars with dilution from stock comp and with leverage trends. The educational payoff is recognizing when “returning cash” is really “re-timing optics.”
