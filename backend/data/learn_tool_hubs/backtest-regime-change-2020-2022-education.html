Equilima — Backtest

Key takeaways

  • Stress windows matter: Test 2020 liquidity crash and 2022 rate shock.
  • Correlations jump: Diversification assumptions fail in stress.
  • Past hypothetical: Not predictive.

Snapshot — early April 2026, when hypothetical strategy posts flood social feeds—learn to verify

2020 rewarded liquidity provision and bounce buyers; 2022 punished duration and high multiple growth. A momentum system might have thrived then choked; a mean-reversion system might have died then thrived. If your backtest only includes 2017–2019, you learned about low-vol grinds, not full cycles. Use SPY and QQQ as benchmarks while you stress your toy strategy—ask max drawdown, not max Instagram likes.

This week, whenever someone posts a smooth equity curve, ask which regime funded it.

Bull case — how this lens can help

Regime awareness stops you from assuming the future rhymes perfectly.

You learn to classify environments: rates up/down, vol up/down.

Bear case — where it breaks

Regime labels are obvious in hindsight, fuzzy live.

Even robust rules can underperform for years—patience vs stubbornness is subjective.

Bottom line

Overlay your hypothetical equity curve with a simple VIX or rates chart—note where pain clusters; plan mentally, not from Equilima advice.
