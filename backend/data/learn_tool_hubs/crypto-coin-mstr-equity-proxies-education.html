Snapshot — early April 2026, when crypto equity proxies and BTC volatility still spill into risk sentiment

When BTC rallies, crypto-related equities often jump faster—and fall faster—because they combine spot beta with business risk. COIN revenue mixes trading fees, interest on stablecoins, and international expansion costs that do not move lockstep with a Saturday night candle. Treasury-heavy stories add financial engineering literacy requirements: understand convertibles, dilution, and how accounting choices interact with volatile assets.

This week, if equity indices wobble, watch whether crypto proxies lead or lag BTC—that relationship teaches you who is forced to de-risk and when.
