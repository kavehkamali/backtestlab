Snapshot — early April 2026 tape: breadth, rates, and sector rotation in the headlines

U.S. investors often ignore FX until it punches EPS. A stronger dollar can compress reported international revenue for multinationals; a weaker dollar can flatter it—accounting is messy and hedging varies. EFA-style international equity exposure adds currency beta U.S. indices hide. DXY is an index, not a trade recommendation—use it to contextualize headlines.

This week, if geopolitical headlines move FX, check whether your stock story is fundamentally domestic or global before blaming ‘random volatility.’
